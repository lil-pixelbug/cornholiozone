Character Profiles

Beavis

Full Name: Beavis

Voiced by: Mike Judge

Personality: Hyperactive, immature, and often clueless. Known for his high-pitched laugh and wild antics.

Alter Ego: Becomes "Cornholio" when he gets really excited, leading to chaotic behavior.

Music: Obsessed with heavy metal, particularly Metallica.

Relationships: Best friends with Butt-Head; speaks of his mother, Shirley Beavis.

Catchphrase: "I am Cornholio! I need TP for my bunghole!"
