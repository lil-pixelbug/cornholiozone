Character Profiles

Beavis

Full Name: Beavis

Voiced by: Mike Judge

Personality: Hyperactive, immature, and often clueless. Known for his laugh ("mheh heh heh or heh heh heh")

Alter Ego: Becomes "Cornholio" when he gets consumes too much sugar.

Music: Obsessed with heavy metal, particularly Metallica.

Relationships:Butt-Head: Best friends with Butt-Head. Shirley Beavis: His mother who is sometimes spoken of. Owns the house that Beavis and Butt-Head are seen in.

Catchphrase: "I am Cornholio! I need TP for my bunghole!"
